The Best Movies And Series.

My Top Three Films.

The Walking Dead

It's a zombie film that focuses not only in fictious and sci-fi scenes but also expresses the importance of mutual understanding, mutual support, mutual love, mutual growth and the ultimate survival.

The Lost

A plane crash happens in a mysterious island coercing the survivors to live as a big family and join forces ready to deal with whatever is oblivious to their expectations.

The Boy Who Harnessed The Wind

An Afrikan boy is forced to apply his love and knowledge for science and innovations to alleviate water access in their village's well.
